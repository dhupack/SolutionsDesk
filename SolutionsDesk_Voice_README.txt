SolutionsDesk Voice — how to use
================================

1. Unzip this folder anywhere (e.g. your Desktop). Keep SolutionsDesk.exe and
   backend.txt together in the same folder.

2. Double-click SolutionsDesk.exe.
   - The first time, Windows may show "Windows protected your PC".
     Click "More info" -> "Run anyway". (This is normal for new apps.)

3. A small floating window opens and stays on top of your call.

4. During a call (Teams / Zoom / Google Meet / phone-through-PC):
   - Choose the mode button:  "Me + call" = hears you AND the other person,
                              "Me only"  = hears only you.
   - Click  > Start ,  speak your question (or let the client speak),
     then click  Stop .
   - The transcript appears. Click "Send" to get the answer in the window.

That's it. You don't need any keys, settings, or accounts.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
For the administrator only
---------------------------------------------------------------------
- backend.txt holds the server address. Put your deployed Render URL on one
  line, e.g.  https://solutionsdesk.onrender.com  (no trailing slash).
- To capture the other side of the call, the call audio must play through the
  computer's default speakers/headset (the Windows default output device).
